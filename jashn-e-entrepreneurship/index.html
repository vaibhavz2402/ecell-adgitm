About Jashn-e-Entrepreneurship

Be the part of grand event of Internship Fair 2019 to be organized by E-Cell ADGITM. "Absence of ambitions, annul the potential", these words are not mere utterances but a message to live by.

Ecell ADGITM takes great pride in inviting all the hustlers, wannabes, and the entrepreneurs of the future to a life-altering experience on 15th October.

Due to the tremendous amount of appreciation garnered by Jashn-e-Entrepreneurship, Ecell plans to reach the horizon by introducing Jashn e Entrepreneurship 2.0. This time we wish to showcase the lives and struggles of 5 maestros of their fields and let them interact in an unrestricted talk show format.

Mark the date 15th October, 11:30 am onwards, ensure that your presence in an environment where ambitions are nurtured
